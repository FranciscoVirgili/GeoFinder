How to Get Started as a Geologist on GeoFinder!

1

Create your profile

Start by creating a detailed profile. Share your experience, areas of expertise, certifications, and any additional skills that set you apart. This will help companies understand what services you offer.

2

Receive project invitations

Once your profile is complete, companies looking for geological services will be able to find you. You will receive invitations to submit proposals based on your skills and their project needs.

3

Send proposals and discuss details

When you receive an invitation, you can send your proposal outlining how you would approach the project. You can discuss project details directly through our platform, ensuring smooth communication.

4

Complete the project and get reviewed

Once hired, work with the company to complete the project. Afterward, the company will leave a review based on your performance. Positive reviews will help you build a strong reputation on GeoFinder.

1

Find the right professional for your project

Geological Services are listed here. When you need geological services, simply post the details of your project. Our platform connects your request with geologists and related professionals who match your needs.

2

Review the proposals

Interested professionals will send you their proposals. You can choose who to hire based on their profiles, experience, and reviews from previous clients.

3

Manage the project directly from the platform and your phone

Once you choose a professional, you can communicate with them, manage the project, schedule visits through our platform, all in one place.

4

Leave a review

After the project is completed, you can leave a review of the professional's work. This helps other users make informed decisions and strengthens the professional's reputation within our community.
