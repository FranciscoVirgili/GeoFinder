How GeoFinder works!

1

Sign In and fill out the form

Fill in your contact details and share your professional experience. Whether you’re a geologist with unique skills or a company seeking specialized talent, here you’ll find your next key partner in geological projects.

2

Review the proposals

Interested professionals will send you their proposals. You can choose who to hire based on their profiles, experience, and reviews from previous clients.

3

Manage the project directly from the platform and your phone

Once you choose a professional or a company, you can communicate with them, manage the project, schedule visits through our platform, all in one place.

4

Leave a review

After the project is completed, you can leave a review of the professional's work. This helps other users make informed decisions and strengthens the professional's reputation within our community.
